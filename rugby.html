About Rugby

Rugby is a fantastic team sport that originated in England. It involves two teams trying to score points by carrying or kicking an oval-shaped ball into the opponent's goal.

Playing rugby helps develop physical strength, endurance, and teamwork skills. It teaches players to work together, communicate effectively, and make quick decisions on the field.

Rugby is known for its energetic and fast-paced nature, and it can be enjoyed by both boys and girls of all ages.
