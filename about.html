About Us

Welcome to SportKidz!

At SportKidz, we are dedicated to getting your child fit and healthy, we provide expert knowledge on a wide range of sports and are dedicated to ensuring every child has a chance to experience sport. We wholeheartedly believe that sport canchange young peoples lives for the better, improving physical and mental health.

Our team of experts is passionate about sports. We teach children from all over australia how to play sports and encourage kids to continue playing sport, we offer a wide range of sports, such as athletics, basketball, soccer, and rugby.

We prioritise making sport a fun, enjoyable experience for kids and educating them about the benefits of staying physically and mentally fit.
