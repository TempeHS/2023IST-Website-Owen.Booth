About Basketball

Basketball is a popular sport played worldwide. It involves two teams competing to score points by shooting a ball through the opponent's hoop.

Playing basketball helps improve hand-eye coordination, agility, and cardiovascular fitness. It also teaches important skills such as teamwork, communication, and strategic thinking.

Basketball can be played both indoors and outdoors, making it accessible and enjoyable for kids of all ages and skill levels.
