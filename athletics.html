About Athletics

Athletics involves various track and field events that showcase an individual's strength, speed, and agility.

Participating in athletics helps improve overall fitness, develop discipline, and instill a competitive spirit. Events can include running, jumping, throwing, and hurdling.

Athletics provides opportunities for athletes to compete at different levels, from school competitions to national and international championships.
